Help

Switch View Button – toggles between Actual and Predictive Grade.

Actual Grade: The grade that you currently have on the class based on the information you have logged.

Predictive Grade: Lets you change the scores on assignments and add or delete anything within the course without penalty. All changes made will be reverted when changing back to “Actual Grade” or going back to the home page.

Green Grade Button – toggles between Running and Total Grade.

Running Grade: Grade based on the weight of all the assignments that you have in currently

Total Grade: Grade based on a weight of 100% total instead of just the assignments you have in.

Adding a New Course: Go to the home page, type in the name of a course and click “Add.”

Adding a New Section: Click on one of your courses, type in the name of a section and its weight, then click “Add.”

Adding a New Item in a Section: Click on one of the sections in your courses, type in the name of the item, the score you got, and click “Add.”

Deleting: Swipe left on what you want to delete and click “Yes”
